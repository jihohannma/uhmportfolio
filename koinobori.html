  • Koinobori

  • Tokyo Midtown’s outdoor exhibition showcases koinobori designs from around the world, celebrating the tradition of carp-shaped windsocks flown on Children’s Day (May 5) to wish for children’s health and growth. Inspired by the Korean folktale Byeoljubujeon, my koinobori depicts the journey between land and sea, following a loyal turtle and a clever rabbit. It illustrates the turtle’s mission to capture the rabbit and the rabbit’s cunning escape, reflecting themes of wit, survival, and deception.

    Year: 2024
    Skills: Illustrator
