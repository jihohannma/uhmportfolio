  • Reminisce: MITATE WORLD – Exhibition

  • Reminisce is a proposed exhibition showcasing the work of Tanaka Tatsuya, a Japanese miniature photographer and mitate artist. Rooted in the traditional concept of mitate, which employs visual puns and reinterpretations of familiar objects, the exhibition invites viewers to explore new perspectives through miniature scenes. The title “Reminisce” reflects this theme of shifting viewpoints and subtly references the word “mini.”
