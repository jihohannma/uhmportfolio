My name is Jiho Han, and I’m a graphic designer with a BFA in Graphic Design from the University of Hawai‘i at Mānoa. My work spans brand identity, UI/UX, print, and digital design—with a focus on thoughtful visuals that communicate clearly and purposefully. Originally from Korea and now based in Hawai‘i, I’ve grown up navigating between cultures, which shaped the way I approach design. I’m drawn to visual storytelling that reflects identity, space, and meaning. I see design as a visual language that transcends words—a medium for connection and communication across different audiences. Whether I’m building a brand system or refining typography, I aim to create work that feels intentional, honest, and human-centered.
