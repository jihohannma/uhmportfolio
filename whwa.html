  • As the Wahiawā Health and Wellness Association transitioned from a hospital to a community-focused wellness organization, they sought a new identity that honors their legacy while supporting their future goals. This logo, titled Kahua (meaning “foundation” in Hawaiian), reflects WHWA’s deep roots in the Wahiawā community and their ongoing commitment to health, growth, and cultural connection.
